For example, a Wish spell could undo an opponent's successful save, a foe's critical hit, or a friend's failed save. You might be able to achieve something beyond the scope of the above examples. State your wish to the GM as precisely as possible. The GM has great latitude in ruling what occurs in such an instance; the greater the Wish, the greater the likelihood that something goes wrong.
